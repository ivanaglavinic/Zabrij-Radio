DOXIA

For Doxia, rave culture is a sacred ritual, a spiritual experience where the rhythm of the night connects us. Her sets blend underground techno, trance, and house with emotional depth, often infused with jungle, UK bass, and electro for a fresh and unpredictable sound. Her beats have echoed at numerous events and festivals, as well as through her past collective Schlag, which provided a safe space for the LGBTIQ community. Her latest project, the her/etik party series in Zagreb, redefines the dance floor as a place of connection. Beyond the club scene, she collaborates with London-based fashion artist Marko Vrbos, curating DJ sets for video art projects. She has also led DJ workshops for women in partnership with the Zbeletron collective, fostering a supportive and inclusive community.
