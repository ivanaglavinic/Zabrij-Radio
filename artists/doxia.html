DOXIA

For Doxia, rave culture is sacred ritualistic and spiritual experience where the rhythm of the night connects us to ourselves and to each other. Her sets are a fusion of underground techno, trance, and house, infused with an emotional depth that resonates with her audience. She is also known to weave in elements of jungle, UK bass, and electro, keeping her sound fresh and unpredictable. Her beats echoes at many events and festivals or through her past collective Schlag which was all about creating a safe haven for the LGBTIQ community, where everyone can lose themselves in the music without fear of judgment. Her latest venture, the her/etik party series in Zagreb, embodies her vision of a dance floor that’s more than just a place to move—it's a platform for connection. Her influence extends beyond the club scene; she is currently collaborating with London-based fashion artist Marko Vrbos, curating a DJ sets for a video art projects and in addition to her work behind the decks, she has led DJ workshops for women, breaking down barriers and fostering a supportive community collaborating with Zbeletron collective.
