SKOLS

Skols is a musician from Zagreb, Croatia. He started making music with his friends while in high school. His sound is often characterized by deep basslines and rich synths, with a swing feel on the drums. His style ranges from deep house to disco. Since 2016, he has been hosting Lagana Srijeda events at Depo Klub and, together with his friend DJ Bekspin, co-hosts House Control events. While DJ-ing, Skols realized he could blend various sounds seamlessly, a method he now applies in his music production.
